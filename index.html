Welcome to C.R Enterprises

C.R Enterprises is a Specialist Aluminium and Glass Contractor with over 20 Years experience in the design & installation of high performance products. Mr Rajeev Sharma, the C.E.O of the company started the company in 1995. Since then the company has grown to acquire the reputation of being one of the leading specialist aluminium and glass contractor present in the industry.

It has successfully completed more than 30 projects with a large pool of clientele, architects, consultants, suppliers and associates. Our expertise on large-scale construction projects provides our customers with the confidence and resources they need to create the best possible façade solutions, utilising a variety of curtain wall products. At C.R Enterprises, we look at each project we are involved with as an opportunity to build a long and mutually beneficial relationship with our customers through delivering our work right first time, on time, every time. Our company is located in the heart of Navi Mumbai. Its lucrative locations provide it a good opportunity to work in various areas such as Mumbai, Pune and Navi Mumbai itself. C.R Enterprises constantly strives to practise international standards with a focus on innovation, customer needs, services, commitments and safety policy.

Services

We are a specialist cladding contractor that transforms architect's vision into reality. We design, fabricate and install modern architectural building envelopes. The company brings its Know-How and expertise to all projects, in particular when dealing with Special Features Buildings, beginning with the design development phases all the way to the successful completion, achieving the customer's expectations. We provide a wide range of services including Windows - All types, Stick Cutrain Wall, Ventilated Fasades, Suspended Glazing, Metal Cladding and Cable net Facades.
